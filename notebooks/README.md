# Principles of Bonds Valuation:

The aim of this notebook is to give walkthrough calculations for: 
- the fair value of a bond
- the construction of the discounting curve
- the total return of a bond under different scenarios

Please note that the modules developed in this project concern only US government Treasury bills which assume no risk of default, no liquidity risk and no optionality. Further enhancements to include corporate bonds and optionality will be implemented later.

For definitions of the terms used in this notebook, please refer to the glossary in the document [01_Basics_Bond_Valuation.md](../documentation/01_Basics_Bond_Valuation.md).
