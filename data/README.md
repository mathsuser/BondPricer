# Par Yield Curve Data

This dataset contains US Treasury par yield curve rates downloaded from the official US Treasury website:
https://home.treasury.gov/resource-center/data-chart-center/interest-rates/daily-treasury-rate-archives

- Source: US Department of the Treasury
- Date range: 1990 to 2023
- Tenors: 1M, 3M, 6M, 1Y, 2Y, 3Y, 5Y, 7Y, 10Y, 20Y, 30Y

For research and educational use only.
